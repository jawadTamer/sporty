ABOUT US

Sporty was established in 2016, opening its first store that same year. Since then, the company has worked with a large variety of clubs and teams. With this, it's growing extensively every year and sponsoring many top-level athletes, professional and rising in the junior ranking.

This list includes: Handball, Basketball first team, Sporting Club first team, Sporting Club youth and Schools such as Schutz American School.

MISSION STATEMENT

Sporty plans on giving 100 million people the ability to wear top-quality clothing at affordable prices by focusing on highlighting the country's ability to show off its rich history.
To grow a successful business by consistently providing the best possible selection of high-quality products for sports and outdoor activities, the best possible level of customer service, and products that are tailored to our customers' changing needs.
To increase our customers' quality of life by promoting the ideals of healthy lifestyle, exercise, and outdoor recreation in order to contribute to the improvement of the nation's health.
